# Polispectra
A visualization tool for political sentiment

## Core Features

### The collective spectra landing page
A simple interface for ordering the subjective importance of various biological systems

When you land on the page you are presented with a question: 

Are you more important than the world?

### the spectral interface
Regardless of your answer to the previous question you are brought to a page with a series of questions.
The user may go back and forth between the questions and re answer as desired.
They must answer at least three questions to submit their "philosophy" to the larger pool and observe others' data

### whats is a philosophy?
a philosophy is a partially ordered set which consists of pairwise relationships between the following scales (which otherwise have an intrinsic canonical linear ordering)

### deduction on scales:
- the self
  - the mind
  - the tissue
  - the body (including non-human organisms)
- the relatives
  - ancestors (grandparents and beyond)
  - your parents
  - your siblings and cousins
  - your spouse and chosen family
  - your progeny and adoptive family
- the others
  - members of your neighborhood
  - members of your metropolitan area
  - members of your province
  - members of your nation
- the systems
  - your occupation
  - your faith
  - your government
  - your international alliences
  - humankind
  - your planet

### Observing others
An interface to see various representations of the partial orderings and the "title" others have submitted them under
